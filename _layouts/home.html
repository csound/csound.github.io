Csound is a sound and music computing system that has its roots in the earliest of computer software, the MUSIC-N series by Max Mathews. Csound was originally released in 1986 by Barry Vercoe and it has been a part of the computer music world since. Today’s Csound works on desktop, mobile, embedded, server, and web platforms and powers music software and music-making for musician’s around the world.

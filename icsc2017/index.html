4th International Csound Conference

Montevideo

Sep. 29 ‒ Oct. 1, 2017

The Fourth International Csound Conference – ICSC2017 will take place in Montevideo, Uruguay, from September 29 to October 1, 2017.

The ICSC is an artistic and academic event where members of the international Csound community meet for three days of concerts, paper presentations, keynote talks and round tables. The full conference program is available here.

Everyone is cordially invited to submit proposals for papers and music, or to simply join us for this gathering.

The ICSC2017 is organized by eMe (Estudio de Música Electroacústica de la Escuela Universitaria de Música — University School of Music Electronic Music Studio) in collaboration with the GPA (Grupo de Procesamiento de Audio — Audio Processing Group).

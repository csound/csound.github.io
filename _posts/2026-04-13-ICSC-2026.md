8th International Csound Conference (ICSC 2026)  
Chiostro di San Domenico, Trapani, Sicily, Italy  
20–22 October 2026  
[https://csound.com/icsc2026/](https://csound.com/icsc2026/)

Submissions are now open for papers and artistic works.

We invite submissions of research papers, music and installations encompassing all aspects of Csound.

The conference is hosted by the Conservatorio di Musica A. Scontrino di Trapani, in collaboration with the Conservatorio di Musica A. Scarlatti di Palermo.

The ICSC is a meeting point for the global Csound community, where artists and researchers share their work through concerts, paper presentations, keynote talks and round tables.

Important Dates

Submission deadline: June 30, 2026 (23:59 Rome time)  
Notification of acceptance: July 22, 2026  
Camera-ready submission: September 15, 2026  
Conference dates: October 20–22, 2026

Further details are available on the [conference website](https://csound.com/icsc2026/).
